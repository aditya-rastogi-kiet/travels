Travel Tips

1. Pack Light but Smart

Always carry essentials like medicines, chargers, ID proofs, emergency cash, and travel-size items.

2. Stay Hydrated During Travel

Long journeys can drain energy. Keep water handy and avoid heavy meals during travel.

3. Research Local Culture

Understanding local customs helps in respectful and safe travel experiences.
